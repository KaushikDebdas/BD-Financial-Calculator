Classification Policy (editable — match your organization's actual rules)
Defaults below follow Bangladesh Bank BRPD Circular No. 15 (2024). If your organization uses different overdue cutoffs, provisioning %, or books income/suspense differently, change them here — this Classification tab and the Loan Accrual (Date Range) tab both read these same settings, so they can never silently disagree with each other.
Ranges: UC = 0 months · SMA = 1 to SMA-cutoff · SS = (SMA-cutoff+1) to SS-cutoff · DF = (SS-cutoff+1) to DF-cutoff · BL = beyond DF-cutoff.
